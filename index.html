Cooking Class Timetable

We have 2 great October half term cooking workshops planned using some fabulous seasonal ingredients.

Awesome Autumnal Bakes - The very best the season has to offer - we will be making one sweet recipe, one savoury recipe and one side recipe.

Tuesday 27th October
10.30am - 12.30pm

Spooktacular Halloween Cooking - Some tasty treats with a frightening twist! We will be making a selection of savoury and sweet recipes, perfect to celebrate Halloween.

Thursday 29th October
10.30am - 12.30pm

All workshops cost £35 each.

They are to be held in West Wickham in one of our leader's home and are suitable for ages 8-15 years.

All classes include equipment, ingredients, PDF recipe sheets and containers to take food home in. All recipes can be made gluten or dairy free if required.

If you would like to book, please get in touch to register your interest and be added to the waiting list. Classes will only run if there are enough participants - maximum of six places.

Bespoke group sessions

I am also more than happy to run bespoke group sessions for you and your family or your children and their friends. These will be £150 for a 2 hour session, with up to 4, additional cost of £25 per person to a maximum of 6 participants at a time and day to suit you.
