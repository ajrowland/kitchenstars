Are you Interested in Summer Holiday Cooking Classes for your Children?

As many of you know I've been taking a break from cooking classes to concentrate on my Nutrition Degree. My semester has come to an end for the summer, and like many of you my holiday will either be cancelled or postponed.

I've really missed the fun of classes and the joy of teaching new recipes. Whilst doing my studies I came across an interesting bit of research by The university of Glasgow. It found that around four, 2 hour cooking sessions can have a long-term impact on healthy eating. That's quite surprising definitely food for thought!

Over the holidays I would love to cook with your children (small groups of 5 - socially distanced classes) which hopefully will help them to develop long-term healthy eating habits whilst having fun! How cool would that be?
